A New PO Role

How can the Product Owner add value and remove waste?

  1. Consider benefits for the end user - 20 votes
  2. Facilitate mutual trust between PO and Devs - 16 votes
  3. Feel comfortable advising the client on an alternative approach/solution - 12 votes
  4. Clear vision of client goals and requirements - 9 votes
  5. Solid acceptance criteria - 6 votes

How could the PO role be improved at River?

  1. Early input at pitch/design stage (21)
  2. Collaborative story writing with devs (11)
  3. Remove hierarchy structure (9)
  4. PO Retros -> sharing knowledge & experience (8)
  5. More PO autonomy to make decisions (7)

What could the new PO role look like?

  • A professional PO; itâ€™s their sole responsibility
  • Sits with the development team(s)
  • Goes out to see customers regularly
  • Is involved in pitches
  • Ensures jobs are economic
  • Reports directly to Phil, since they are neither account or dev, but bridge the divide
  • Gets together with the other POs to share knowledge and benefit from each other's experience

What type of person would be suited?

  • Visionary and Doer
  • Leader and Team Player
  • Communicator and Negotiator
  • Empowered and Committed
  • Potentially any level - we will have a mix in the team

The day to day

  • Crafting User Stories
  • Working with Customers 10%-20% time
  • Working with Developers to find solutions
  • Working with Designers to build a better User Experience
  • Working with Phil and the Comms team to help shape what we pitch
  • Designing components that can be reused across solutions

Where does it leave the Account Teams?

  • More time to focus on the customer
  • No more story writing!
  • Work side by side with the PO's to help shape the solution
  • Pass on invaluable feedback from customers
  • Share ideas about client's needs
