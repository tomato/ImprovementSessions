The Dunbar Number

Professor Robin Dunbar suggests that 150 is the maximum number of people with whom one can maintain stable relationships

Our success depends on the ability to create cooperative relationships within and across teams.

What gets in the way of cooperative relationships at work?

Identifying the "Us and "Thems" at River

  • Which roles are the "Us" and "Thems"? What type of things do you blame each other for?
  • Create a poster illustrating these relationships

Blame Role Play!

  • Select an Us and Them that you feel would benefit from close collaboration
  • Split each team into 2. One side be the Us, the other the Them
  • Now blame each other for all your problems!

Where does the blame come from?

Not treating "thems" as real people

What happens to our problems when we blame?

We remove the possiblity of helping to fix them

What can we do to repair the relationships?

Replace Blame with Enquiry

  • What are their frustrations? How can you help
  • Replace Judgement with Empathy?
  • Openness: Explain your frusration and needs.

Repair your relationship

  • Return to you Role Play and practice the techniques we've discussed to repair the relationship
